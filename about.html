About Us

Who We Are

Green Line Oasis LLP

Welcome to Green Line Oasis LLP, Aurangabad's dedicated innovator in sustainable poultry by-product recycling. We specialize in producing high-quality poultry by-product meals and poultry oil, backed by a commitment to responsible processing and environmental care.

Our facility is built around a scientific approach to collecting and processing poultry slaughter by-products. Every step — from raw material intake to final packaging — is managed with precision, hygiene, and consistency at the core of our operations.

We are having a Rendering plant with premium quality machinery using European technology, operating as per EU norms to manufacture premium high quality products with high protein and high digestibility.
