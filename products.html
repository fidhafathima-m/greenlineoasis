Our Products

What We Offer

Premium by-product solutions

Green Line Oasis LLP produces two core products — both derived from rigorously processed poultry by-products and meeting the highest industry standards for quality, hygiene, and consistency.

Product 01

Pet Grade Poultry Byproduct Meal

Our Pet Grade Poultry Byproduct Meal is a superior source of nutrition carefully formulated for use in pet food and animal feed. With a protein content of up to 65% and controlled levels of ash, fat, and moisture, it offers a balanced and enriching dietary supplement.

Presented in a solid, powder form with a light to mid-brown colour, our poultry byproduct meal carries a natural aroma of poultry meat, free from rancidity. The product undergoes treatment to eliminate salmonella and other pathogenic agents, ensuring full purity at the point of dispatch.

Packaged in bulk or in 50 kg bags and stored in clean, ventilated conditions at room temperature, the product maintains a shelf life of 12 months from the date of manufacture.

Key Specifications
Protein ContentUp to 65%
FormSolid / Powder
ColourLight to Mid-Brown
PackagingBulk / 50 kg bags
Shelf Life12 months
Pathogen TreatmentSalmonella-free
Product 02

Poultry Oil

Green Line Oasis LLP's poultry oil is an essential source of lipids used across the animal feed industry and related sectors. Derived from carefully collected and processed raw materials sourced from inspected slaughterhouses, our poultry oil reflects our commitment to quality and sustainability.

The oil presents as a clear liquid with distinct yellowish tones and adheres to the highest hygiene standards. It is free from salmonella and other pathogenic agents, achieved through strict temperature control throughout the manufacturing process.

Supplied in bulk and stored in appropriate metallic tanks to minimise environmental impact, our poultry oil has a shelf life of 12 months from the date of production. It is widely used in animal feed production, and the growing biofuel segment.

Key Specifications
FormClear Liquid
ColourYellowish
StorageBulk / Metallic Tanks
Shelf Life12 months
Pathogen TreatmentSalmonella-free
ApplicationsAnimal Feed, Biofuel
